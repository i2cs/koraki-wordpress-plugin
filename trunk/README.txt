Koraki, AI powered social proof widget for Wordpress/WooCommerce

== Description ==
There are many potential customers visiting your website but leave it because they don't believe what you say. Building customer trust is the key point for better conversion rate. You have less than 1 minute to gain their trust and Koraki is here to help you. Now you can turn your website visitor behavior patterns to lead generating notifications and sales pops with advanced AI algorithms of Koraki

== Installation ==

1. Upload `koraki.zip` through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
2. Activate the plugin after upload completes
